Arun Raj is one of the software developers focusing on Immersive Experiences at IdentityMine, an outstanding interactive design, software development, and user experience (UX) agency. During his time at IdentityMine he has worked on projects across a variety of platforms from XBOX 360, Windows 8.1, Xamarin - Android, WPF and Windows 10. He's worked as part of Core Development team, Design Integration team and Immersive experiences team across the globe to ensure client needs are aligned visually and technically. He is based in Cochin and blogs about many topics of HoloLens, Mobile, MR and Windows development in general and writes articles.
